Cuban Sandwich

Description

Not Cuban.

Ingredients

  • Ehm... cuban... bread.
  • Some mayo.
  • Some mustard.
  • Some cayenne pepper... I've no idea.
  • SWISS cheese, not Cuban for some reason.
  • Six (6, 六) thin slices of thinly smoked thinly-yet-fully-cooked thin ham.
  • Some cooked pork, right.
  • Definitely NOT some Cuban chicken.
  • More thin stuff, this time it's pickle.
  • Two (2, 二) tablespoons of butter, or more...?

How To Make That Juicy “Cuban” Sandwich

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Undo -gather ingredients-.
  3. Gather ingredients once more.
  4. Mix mayo, mustard and cayenne into a sauce.
  5. Divide bread in half longitudinally.
  6. Spread sauce on bread.
  7. Put rest of ingredients in bread.
  8. Melt butter in pan using your kitchen's stove's fire or electrical heating system. If Fire-Wifi connectivity available use it.
  9. Toast bread on both sides.
  10. Eat with mouth.

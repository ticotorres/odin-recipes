Homemade Lasagna

Description

Someone's mom's homemade lasagna recipe. It's got a meaty tomato sauce MADE FROM STRATCH and a lot of cheese. Don't dare to think it'll get better than that.

Now, I know what you're thinking: BuT wHaT iS sO sPecIaL aBoUt ThIs LaSagNa?

The answer is in the cheese. While old boring traditional lasagnas are made with ricotta cheese, this one is cheesed up with a blend of small-curd cheese and Parmesan. Bet you didn't see that coming.

Anyways, here's the ingredients you'll be needing:

Ingredients

  • Ground meat: lots of it.
  • Onion: for the tasty tears.
  • Canned tomatoes: lots of it.
  • Fresh herbs.
  • Sugar (just a tiny bit, it isn't cake what we're making).
  • Cheese: we already discussed this. But also, add mozzarella in there.
  • Eggs. How many? Who knows!

How To Cook that Lasagnina

  1. Cook meat with canned tomato (be sure to remove tomato from can).
  2. Boil the lasagna pasta sheets in BOILING WATER.
  3. Mix them cheeses together.
  4. Assemble the lasagna.
  5. Oven time until golden brown or till it burns.
